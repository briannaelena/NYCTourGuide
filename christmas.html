Rockefeller Center Christmas Tree Lighting

The Rockefeller Center Christmas Tree Lighting is one of the most breathtaking events in NYC. It always takes place in midtown Manhattan and it's been a tradition since 1933. This event inlcudes perfomers and the thousands of people that attend, as well as the millions that watch the event on their TVs. The tree lighting puts people in the holiday spirit and represents the inner and outer beauty of the city.

When:

It will be lit on Wednesday, November 28, 2018 between 7:00PM - 9:00PM

It will last until 9:00PM on Monday, January 7, 2019

Location:

It's between West 48th and 51st Streets and Fifth and Sixth Avenues
