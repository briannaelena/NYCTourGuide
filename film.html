Tribeca Film Festival

The Tribeca Film Festival showcases a selection of independent films. It gives independent filmmakers a chance to display their work to an audience. This festival catches the attention of about 3 million people. It includes showing documentaries, shorts, family-friendly films, etc. It started in 2002, and it was founded by Jane Rosenthal, Robert De Niro and Craig Hatkoff. If you're a movie fanatic and enjoy culture, this event is for you.
